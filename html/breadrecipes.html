Star Twisted Bread

What Do you need?

  • 1 package (1/4 ounce) Active dry yeast
  • ¼ cup Water
  • ¾ cup Whole milk
  • 1 Large eggs
  • ¼ cup Butter
  • ¼ cup Sugar In The Raw
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 3¼ - 3¾ cups All-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup Raspberry jam
  • 2 tbsp Butter Confectioners' sugar
